Code Greer

Once upon a time there was a girl named Sarah who lived an ordinary life doing ordinary things. But then one day she felt a tiny spark of inspiration.

She decided to learn how to design, build, and style websites with HTML & CSS. Then she studied Javascript to make them interactive and Ruby to understand back end design. This passion for web development started her on a quest for fun and work. She was done with ordinary. It was time to share her knowledge. It was time to be extraordinary!

Sarah designed and built her first website 14 years ago. Shortly after, she put aside her work to take on the challenge of homeschooling her four charming and brilliant children. Recently, she has rekindled her love of learning and is taking on projects that inspire and excite her.
